Call for Proposals

Guidelines

  • Proposal should have a clear title and content description.
  • Proposal description should be brief.
  • Proposals should be sent to proposals@pypals.org
  • Proposal content should adhere to the prescribed code of conduct.
  • Selection about talks will be notified via email.
  • Content of proposed talk should cover a duration of 35 minutes.
  • For any questions, please write to contact@pypals.org.

Important Dates

  • October 6 : Proposal submission deadline (Extended Deadline)
  • October 7 : Talk selection announcement
  • October 10 : Final announcement
